WHY SELF DEFENSE KEYCHAINS?

Self-defense keychains may seem small,but for many women safety is built in small,everyday choices.These choices are something you can carry quietly in your hand ,clip onto your hand reach for in a moment of fear.They are not about violence,they are about readiness,confidence and not giving women a chance to protect themselves when help may not be available.This initiative is not about profit its about diginity,prevention and care .if even one woman feels safer because she chose to carry one , then it is worth it
